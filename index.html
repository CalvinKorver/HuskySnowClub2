Membership Perks

We've built a incredible community of UW students stoked to shred. In addition to our private rideshare, we provide a bunch of other perks that make being a member of Husky Snow Club that much sweeter.

  • Drivers

    Got a car? Great.
    Take up two or three other members and we'll reimburse the gas for your trip. Seriously, we pay cash.

  • Riders

    No car? No problem.
    Our private rideshare connects you with drivers who will get you up to the mountains to do what you love.

  • Ski Trips

    Like the idea of shredding Whistler during the day, hitting the hot tub, then bar-hopping until 2am? We plan some amazing trips that keep people coming back every year.

  • Events & Parties

    We host both members-only & public parties while we wait for the snow to pile up, giving you the chance to meet others who want to shred come winter.

  • Mountain BBQs

    Nothing beats burgers and brats on a beautiful day. Rain or shine, we host on-mountain BBQs that are free for all members!

  • Resources

    New to skiing? Need help finding gear or figuring out passes? We love to help skiers & boarders of all levels. You also have access to our private members page, where you'll find lots of info from our experienced members!
